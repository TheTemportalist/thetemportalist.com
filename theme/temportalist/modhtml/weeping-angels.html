Blocks

Angel Plinth

A statue which comes to life when given any redstone signal (strong or weak).
When right clicked, will open a GUI (Graphical User Interface), which allows the user to enter a rotation of the rendered angel's pitch. Can also be used to specify the state the angel is in. This ranges from Hding, Peaking or Angry arm/wing positions, or Calm or Angry facial expressions.
This block is a rare drop from the Weeping Angel entity, and can be found in Vaults.

Angel's Tear

The Angel's Tear is a semi-rare drop (20% chance) from a Weeping Angel entity.
When dropped, the tear will have 1 of 2 effect types, along with a certain amount of uses. When these uses are up, the Tear will disintegrate.
When combined with a normal vanilla Minecraft Arrow in a crafting table, you will get an Angel Arrow.

Arrow

The Arrow (Sometimes called the "Angel Arrow"), is a special kind of arrow. Fire-able from any Bow, this arrow can be used to frighten your opponent, or troll a player (as per request from Fireball1725).
Uses:

  1. Player Conversion On impact with any other player entity, the arrow will fall apart and the tear ingrained in the arrow's tip will cause the impacted player to start being converted.
  2. Ender Dragon Troll As per Fireball1725 and GlassPelican 's request, we have a special thing! On impact with the Ender Dragon, all Endermen in loaded chunks in the dimension will turn into Weeping Angel entities.

Entities

Weeping Angel

Oh the joy. The joy of terrorizing the players and testificates of Minecraftia. Here is what you need to know about Weeping Angels:

Don't Blink. Whatever you do, Don't Blink. Blink and your dead. They are faster then you can believe. However, take assurance in the fact that they are Quantum locked life forms. They literally, cannot moved while they are being observed. They turn to stone.

OK! So here is the break down!

  • Cannot move while any other player/angel can see them
  • Extremely Fast, when able to move
  • Loves to go after players and either:
    1. Convert them into a Weeping Angel
    2. Teleport them to another 'time'
    3. Kill them
  • Will get old and decrepit over time, causing them to decay
  • When extremely old, will a-ceullularly procreate, spawning a baby angel
    - Baby Angels are 1/2 the height, and twice the speed of a normal angel
  • When teleporting or converting a player (mentioned above), can take some of the player's energy and become slightly younger

Angel Conversion

When a player is infected by a dormant Weeping Angel, they only have so long to live. As the angel inside you grows bigger and stronger, you will find that your sight gets dimmer. By they time the angel is ready to emerge, you will have lost all sight, and hope most likely. At this point, the angel will emerge will full health (20/20), and immediately kill teh player.
Also, when that occurs, the angel has the opportunity to copy your name, and steal any inventory you had at the time, so be careful out there.
PS: There is no way to be cured of an Angel Conversion yet.

World Gen

Vaults

Vaults are special world generated dungeons. They can contain up to 2 loot chests and 1 mob spawner (which spawns Weeping Angels). They are constructed out of various stone-like materials, and hold 7 Plinths.
