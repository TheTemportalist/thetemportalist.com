What do you get back by donating?
Well, the nice thing about this thing called Patreon, are the pledges and milestones. Based on your current donation amount, you will get the rewards detailed below in the pledge area. Global rewards will be achieved on the left under the milestones. These are triggered when I am getting enough donations per month to do something exciting for the community. Each milestone goal has a certain cost I have calculated. Part of the money per milestone goes straight into the goal itself (EX: say the goal was to do go to the movies, part of the cost would go towards the cost of travel and the cost of the theater). The rest of the money goes into the the things specified under "My goal / ...". (EX: the cost of the goal - the amount of money spent on the cost of going to the movie theater = the amount of money put towards my studies/community/sanity)
